## 6. Your Rights

Under Colombian Ley 1581 de 2012 and, where applicable, the EU GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Know** what data we hold about you
- **Update** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your personal data (right to erasure)
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (for newsletter)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured format

To exercise any right: **legal@js17.dev** — we respond within 10 business days.
