These videos were not edited in Premiere Pro. They were not scripted by a copywriter. They were not recorded in a studio.

They were **generated** by an architecture that treats content as code:

1. **GPT-4o** writes narration scripts with structured intro/core/outro
2. **OpenAI TTS** (onyx voice, HD model) generates broadcast-quality audio
3. **Shotstack** composites slides with audio into MP4
4. **YouTube Data API** uploads, categorizes, and adds to playlist
5. **Vercel Blob** tracks every video per article slug

One click. Five services. Under 3 minutes per video.
